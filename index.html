ChiaLinks

ChiaLinks is a curated collection of resources from around the web for all things related to the Chia cryptocurrency (XCH) -- green money for a digital world.

Chia is a blockchain and smart transaction platform that uses disk storage space (Proof of Space and Time) as an energy efficient and democratized means of securing the decentralized network. Chia was created by Bram Cohen, the inventor of the BitTorrent network, and is managed by the Chia Network.
